About RabinVerse

Hello, I'm Rabin Shahi, an MSc Applied Computer Science student. RabinVerse—also known as Freeverse—is inspired by Openverse and designed to democratize access to creative content.

Our mission is to provide an open, accessible space where creators can explore and share a wealth of creative resources. Whether you’re a designer, developer, or creative enthusiast, RabinVerse empowers you to discover and innovate.
